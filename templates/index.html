Welcome to FireWatch

FireWatch predicts wildfires before they happen! Our web app uses machine learning models trained on earth surface and soil data to provide real-time risk assessments with unmatched accuracy. Perfect for forest rangers, firefighters, and anyone who wants to stay informed about wildfire threats in their area. Protect your property and community with FireWatch.

Forest Rangers

Forest rangers play a crucial role in protecting our forests and natural resources from devastating wildfires. With FireWatch, forest rangers can have access to the latest tools and technologies to detect and respond to fires quickly and effectively. By leveraging real-time data on weather conditions, fire activity, and other critical information, FireWatch can help forest rangers stay ahead of potential wildfires, enabling them to take proactive measures to prevent and contain fires before they become unmanageable.

Fire Fighters

Firefighters play a critical role in managing and extinguishing wildfires. With the unpredictable nature of wildfires, firefighters need to have access to the latest tools and technologies to predict the behavior of fires and respond effectively. FireWatch provides firefighters with predictions on the potential spread of wildfires based on data from satellite imagery and weather forecasts. These predictions help firefighters to plan and allocate resources effectively to prevent wildfires from spreading, and to minimize the damage caused by fires.

Property Owners

Property owners in wildfire-prone areas need to be prepared for the possibility of a wildfire. FireWatch's image recognition technology can help property owners detect the possibility of a wildfire based on satellite footage, allowing them to take necessary precautions to protect their homes and families.

Goverment Officials

Government officials have a responsibility to ensure public safety and prevent the spread of wildfires. FireWatch's image recognition technology provides a powerful tool for monitoring and detecting the possibility of a wildfire, enabling officials to take proactive measures to protect communities and prevent property damage.
